Will You Be Redesigning Your Website This Year?



Caption: Re-design for www.krpm.com.my with a green and wide approach.


Web redesign is indeed a very interesting task. I enjoy design and revamping websites with all my heart.

I really enjoy re-designing websites to create the new spark in a new look that can inspires freshness, innovation, creativity and most importantly user-friendliness. Yes, there are too many things just mentioned, hence we need to scale it down. Before worrying about any of the creative or technical details, it's important to approach the process with the right mindset and preparations.

Visit Site

Caption: Re-design for www.henner.com.my with a serious building engineering feel.

I wish to share four tips based on my experiences:

1. Place your visitors at heart. We always have to remember that your new website is designed and tailored for your intended visitors. Your visitors must find your features and functions useful and easy to use. The personal preferences of yours or other executives should not dictate your design. Your brand identity and your targeted visitors should drive the design.

2. Choose one project dedicated person as your project lead. This person should solicit feedback but filter accordingly. However, make sure all decision-makers are in the room during approval meetings. This shall person commit to all deadlines.

3. Find a reputable digital marketing firm or internet solution firm. This makes a huge difference. Evaluate for firms that are flexible and can think out of the box. Of course, a firm that has experience in your industry counts too, but don't let experience be the barrier of innovation or creativity.

4. Have a reasonable budget for copywriting and photography. Great content and professional photography can make the difference. Great images and inspiring contents can make an interesting website.

Good luck and all the best in your web re-design exercise.
