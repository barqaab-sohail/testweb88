How to change the logo in laravel administrator page?
By Danny Foo - October 3rd 2016 17.00 PM GMT+0800

Here is a tip of a faster way to change your logo in WEB88 content management system (CMS). It is pretty fast as it only takes three simple steps and maybe less than three minutes.

Step 1 : Logon to FTP and search for the following folders.

/public_html/web88/admin/images
/public_html/public/admin/images

Step 2 : Look for the image file with the following name.

logo.jpg

Step 3 : Upload your file and replace it by overwriting it.

The size of the image shall be 125px by 54px.
The format of the image shall be jpeg/jpg.
