Recent Launch
November 2010

www.hil.com.my

HIL began operations in Malaysia in 1969 as Hagemeyer Industries (M) Sdn.Bhd., a Dutch Company involved in the trading and manufacturing of 'BIC' ball pens. In 1989, following a management buyout, the name of the Company was changed to Hil Industries Sdn. Bhd.

It was subsequently converted to a public listed company on the 10th of June 1991 and listed on the Second Board of the KLSE on the 28th of January 1992. HIL obtained the ISO 9002 accreditation in 1991 and was in actual fact, the first plastic injection moulder to attain it in Malaysia. HIL has also been an approved manufacturer with Underwriters Laboratories USA since 1980. HIL's main factory is located on a 7 acre site in Jalan Lada Sulah 16/11, Section 16 Shah Alam and houses the injection moulding, mould making, spray painting and metal stamping divisions. One of HIL's subsidiary is also involved in the design and manufacture of customized factory automation equipment for various industries. In 1998, HIL's operations commenced at its second factory in Jalan Bukit Kemuning, Shah Alam.

This factory has a built-up area of over 10,000 square metres and consists of a large assembly plant together with facilities for a unique surface decoration/coating technology as well as blow molding facilities. HIL's first foray into overseas market began in 2006 with the setup of its first plant in Suzhou, China. With over 12,000 sq metres of built up factory, this plant offers a one-stop plastic solution provider for the Information Technology industry offering mold making, injection molding, spray painting and various unique decoration technologies as well as sub-assemblies. In 2009, HIL commenced operations at its second plant, also in Suzhou due to increasing demand. And in 2010, HIL commenced operations on its third plant purely for tooling manufacturing.
