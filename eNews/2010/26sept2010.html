www.pitterpatter.com.my

Pitterpatter.com.my was created by Two Obstetricians with the intention of promoting awareness on women's health and pregnancy related issues. Their aim was to create a local community & platform for women & their partners to share their opinions & views on pregnancy, parenthood & challenges of having a family in a hectic modern setting. They believe that effective dissemination of reliable healthcare information and education is an important tool in improving the general healthcare of women and family. Their passion and vision had quickly gained the support from other Obstetricians, Paediatricians & doctors whom shared the same view.
