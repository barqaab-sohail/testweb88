Why Register & Renew For Longer?

The longer you register or renew your domain name for, the longer you have it secured and away from your competitors. Plus, you don't need to worry about renewals so often, just relax instead, everything is taken care of for the next few years.

Securing your domain for longer can also benefit you financially, as many longer-term registrations and renewals work out cheaper per year than the standard 1 or 2-year periods.
