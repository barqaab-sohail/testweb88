Co-location Servers

Choose what's right for you.

Easily manage your own IT environment without the hassles of having to look after your own data centre. Co-locate your servers in our Cold Aisle Containment System, a green technology. This system features improved airflow, energy efficient equipment, and controlled environment conditions, such as temperature and humidity, ensuring equipment longevity, better security, and higher productivity.

Webqom offers single server colocation and private colocation, with up to 64 amps of power and connectivity from 1Gbps to 10Gbps.

And if you don't see the solution you're looking for, why not talk to us about providing a colocation solution tailored to your needs.
